Strategy

Spot Holding iUSD Using the Stability Pool.

Step 1 :

Swap ADA for iUSD using the "Swap Tokens" tab in the Indigo web app.

Step 2 :

Deposit iUSD into the stability pool. If the price of Ada decrease against iUSD, liquidations of CDPs will occur.
iUSD is then burned in the stability pool to repay debts of CDPs.

  • Earn ADA from Liquidations
  • Earn INDY Rewards

Step 3 :

Wait for the ADA price to decrease to your desired level.

Step 4 :

Swap iUSD for ADA using the "Swap Tokens" tab in the Indigo web app.
